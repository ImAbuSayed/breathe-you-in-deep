[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Come a little closer, let the shadows fall
I want to read the secret on your soul
We have nothing but the moonlight on the floor
And I could stay right here forevermore

[Verse 1]
The way you look at me across the room
Like honey dripping sweet into the gloom
I feel the fire sparking in my chest
You wear that midnight kind of gorgeous best

[Pre-Chorus]
Take my hand and pull me in
Let the quiet slowly fade
Every time I trace your skin
I forget the world we made

[Chorus]
I just want to breathe you in deep
Caught inside a feeling, wild and sweet
Hold you close until the morning breaks
You are the only promise I will make

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Breathe you in deep)
Yeah, you are everything I need

[Verse 2]
I memorize the curve along your smile
I want to get lost with you a little while
There is a heavy magic in the air
My fingers tangled up inside your hair

[Pre-Chorus 2]

Let the quiet slowly fade

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Echo] (Breathe you in deep)
Yeah, you are everything I need

[Bridge]
I just want to breathe you in deep
Every single secret yours to keep
Whisper for Saeed, I am yours to find
Let the heavy world fall far behind
You are the only fire that I know
Take a little piece of me and do not let go

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (I just want to hold you close)
[Spoken] (Close your eyes and let me fall)

[Final Chorus]
